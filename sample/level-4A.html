Sample story

You walk to the computer. Its screen glows softly and a comfortable chair materializes in front of it. You sit down and watch as a message prints on the screen… “birdcage - b.” You frown, puzzled. Looking around the computer, you notice a deck of cards. Strangely enough, when you open it, it only contains aces. You think for a moment, then rearrange the letters of “birdcage” in your head. Without the b, the letters form “cridage” or maybe “race dig”... Suddenly the answer hits you. “Ace grid!” you exclaim. But what exactly does that mean?

Do you...

A. Use the aces to form a grid on the computer.

B. Type in “ace grid” onto the computer.
