Sample story

You get up and open the window. Cold air rushes in and you shiver, but you forget about the cold when you see what was making the chirping sound. A mechanical bird is sitting on your windowsill, its black eyes shining in the moonlight. It opens its mouth and chirps again, then a small red button lights up on its back. You squint and read the words around it: “push in case of emergency.”

Do you...

A. Push the button. You’re curious what it’ll do.

B. Not push the button. It’s not an emergency, is it?
