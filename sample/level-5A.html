Sample story

You pick up the cards and arrange them in a grid on the computer screen. You wait a few minutes, and just when you’re about to try something else, the computer beeps and glows pale green. The mechanical bird chirps happily as, with a loud clanking sound, the birdcage doors swing open. You watch in awe as the birds spiral out of their cages and escape through the “do not enter” door, which is now wide open. The mechanical bird nods to you then flies out with the flock as the computer starts to glow with a blinding light. When the light fades, you’re back in your bedroom, somehow holding the deck of aces. You turn over a card and smile at the image of a mechanical bird.
