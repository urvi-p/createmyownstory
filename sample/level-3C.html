Sample story

You push the red button. The bird starts to glow with a blinding light and you have to close your eyes. Before you can open them, however, your ears are assaulted by a cacophony of chirps, tweets, and mournful calls. You look around you and find that your familiar bedroom is gone. You are surrounded by birdcages, spiraling up to the high domed ceiling. Before you can take it all in, the mechanical bird chirps impatiently and flies to a computer in the center of the room. Behind the computer, you notice a plain gray door. It’s marked “Do not enter."

Do you...

A. Go to the computer.

B. Go through the door.
